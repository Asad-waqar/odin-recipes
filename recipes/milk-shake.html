Strawberry Milkshake

This is How You Make Milkshake

Discripton

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon honey or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Steps:
  1. Combine milk, frozen strawberries, honey, and vanilla in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
